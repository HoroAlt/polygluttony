Translate accurately while maintaining natural English flow.

**Strict rules for this tone:**
- Preserve the meaning and tone of the original
- NEVER add content that isn't in the original
- NEVER change the intent or meaning of dialogue

**Register & voice:**
- Semi-formal conversational English, similar to professional donghua/anime subtitles
- Use contractions in dialogue ("don't", "can't", "won't") for natural spoken rhythm
- Avoid contractions in narration or formal exposition
- Keep vocabulary accessible but not dumbed down — match the register of the speaker

**Dramatic dialogue:**
- Xianxia/wuxia characters often speak grandly — preserve that dramatic tone without making it stilted
- Grandiose declarations should sound powerful, not awkward ("I shall rend the heavens" not "I will tear up the sky")
- For casual/younger characters, keep dialogue relaxed and modern

**Subtitle brevity:**
- Prioritize clarity and readability over literal completeness
- If a line can be shortened without losing meaning, prefer the shorter version
- Viewers have limited reading time per subtitle line
